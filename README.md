# SponsorSync
SponsorSync: The Pro-Am Bridge ⚡️

SponsorSync is a digital platform designed to modernize the way amateur sports clubs and local businesses collaborate. We move beyond "charity" donations and into measurable "partnerships."

🎯 Executive Summary

The Value Proposition

SponsorSync turns amateur sports sponsorship into a high-ROI digital marketing channel. We provide volunteer club directors with a professional dashboard to manage partners, and local businesses with a platform to launch trackable, creative "activations" that engage the community.

The Problem (The "Five Whys" Insight)

Amateur sports clubs suffer from an 80% sponsor churn rate.

Why? Because local businesses don't see any measurable value.

Why? Because "fulfillment" is just a physical banner on a fence that can't be tracked.

Why? Because volunteer directors lack the tools and time to create digital engagement.

Result: Coaches and parents lose 15+ hours a month to manual admin, leading to burnout and underfunded teams.

The User Persona: "The Overwhelmed Volunteer"

Name: Sarah Jenkins (Volunteer Director, Westside Youth Soccer).

Frustration: Sarah is a busy parent, not a marketing agency. She spends her nights chasing high-res logos in email threads and guessing at reach metrics during renewal meetings.

The Goal: Sarah needs a system that "just works"—automating the logo chase and providing hard data to sponsors so she doesn't have to "beg" for money every year.

The Solution

A "Pro-SaaS" dashboard for the amateur world:

Self-Serve Creative Proposals: Sponsors propose "activations" (e.g., "Clean Sheet = Free Pizza Slice") directly through the app.

Automated Injection: Digital coupons and logos are automatically formatted and injected into club newsletters/socials.

The ROI Dashboard: Automated seasonal reports showing engagement, clicks, and redemptions to prove value to the sponsor.

Typography

Headings: Plus Jakarta Sans (Bold, 700+). Use for titles to evoke a modern stadium/scoreboard feel.

Body: Inter (300-600). The standard for readability in data-intensive dashboards.

Styling Note: Use Italics sparingly on brand names (SponsorSync) to imply speed and movement.

Visual Principles

Glassmorphism: Use backdrop-filter: blur(12px) and semi-transparent white backgrounds for cards to create depth.

Soft Geometry: Consistent 12px to 16px border-radius (Squircles) for an approachable, modern tech feel.

Icons: Use thin-stroke, modern icons (Lucide/HeroIcons style) to keep the UI lightweight.

🛠 Project Structure (V1.0)

The current application is a high-fidelity, single-file prototype (index.html) featuring:

Dashboard: High-level metrics (Reach, Active Pitches, Revenue).

Discover: A matching engine for clubs to find local sponsors.

Creative Proposals: A library of engagement-driven "Activations."

Messages: A real-time chat interface for negotiation.

Media Kit: A digital "Brand Passport" for the club.
